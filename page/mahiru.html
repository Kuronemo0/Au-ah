Shiina Mahiru

She is idolized at school because of her beauty as well as her academic and athletic abilities, and is given the nickname of Angel. Her name, Mahiru (noon), which was given by her father, Asahi (morning sun), represents the noon in between morning and night, which is the name of her mother, Sayo (little night).
