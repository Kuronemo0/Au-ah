Krull Tepes

Krul Tepes (クルル・ツェペシ, Kururu Tsepeshi) is the vampire queen of Japan. She resides in the Third Capitol of the vampires Sanguinem, and also is the Third Progenitor, making her the strongest vampire in Japan. Deep down, she resents being a vampire, and the First Progenitor for turning her into one.
