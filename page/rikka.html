Takanashi Rikka

Rikka is the main female character in the story. Yūta's classmate who lives in her brother's house, which is directly above Yūta's apartment. She is a girl who has severe hallucinations and delusions, and believes that she has the "Wicked Eye" (邪王真眼, Jaō Shingan, Eye of the King of Darkness) .
